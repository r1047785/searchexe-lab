Frequent Asked Questions

General

Where is the club located?

The club is located on the campus de Ham, Raghenoplein 21 bis, 2800 Mechelen.

What is the purpose of this gaming club?

The gaming club is a meeting place for students from the same program and teachers to play games together, organize events, and purchase gaming-related products.

How can I stay updated on club activities and news?

You can subscribe to our newsletter via the website or follow us on social media to stay updated on upcoming events and news.

Can I bring friends to club activities?

Since the club is only accessible to students and teachers, friends unfortunately cannot attend club activities unless they are also enrolled in the same program.
